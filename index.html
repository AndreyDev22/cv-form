Profile

Tony Stark is the adopted son of Howard and Maria Stark. When Howard found out that his wife would have big problems with the birth of the child, he made an alliance with an alien robot to pump up the infant's stamina. Because of the robot's machinations, the child, named Arno, was born smart but sickly.

Howard and Maria sent him to the hospital for treatment and adopted Tony, who was healthy and just as smart. Tony turned out to be a real super-genius (and a rare wreck at that), he went to a prestigious school (where he met Bruce Banner), and after his adoptive parents died in a car accident, Tony inherited their company.
